About Technology

Technology is the application of scientific knowledge to create tools and systems that solve problems.

Uses of Technology

  • Communication (mobile, internet)
  • Education (online learning)
  • Healthcare (modern machines)
  • Business (online shopping)

Advantages

  • Saves time
  • Easy information access
  • Improves communication
  • Supports education

Disadvantages

  • Mobile addiction
  • Privacy issues
  • Cybercrime
  • Health problems
